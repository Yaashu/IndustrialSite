    About Us

    SUPER HYLAM PVT. LTD.



    Super Hylam Pvt. Ltd. is an ISO 9001:2008 Quality Management, ISO 14001:2004 Environment Management, and ISO 18001:2007 Health And Safety Management Company. Established in 1994, It is one of the young, dynamic, professionally managed, and fastest-growing high-pressure laminates manufacturing companies. Engaged in the manufacture and export of industrial and decorative laminates, It has carved a niche for itself in laminates brands in the country, with quality second to none. In addition to the wide assortment of laminates, which is available in varied texture and finishes, the company also offer electric panel, switchboard panels, and industrial laminate boards to satiate the requirements of their clients. They make sure that all the products are passed through stringent quality checks at every stage of the production process. Each Super Hylam product is certified with the Bureau of The Indian Standards – IS 2046:1995. Their laminates are manufactured at a world-class facility equipped with imported machinery and imported design papers made from highly stable and resistant pigments, which guarantee longevity and ensure that the product looks great after years of wear and tear.
