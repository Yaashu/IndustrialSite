More About The Product

Super Hylam compact Decor are made from layers of paper bonded together using specially formulated phenolic and melamine resins having special chemical additives and are graded on the basis of their properties to ensure clear drill holes and minimum of burr during special technical purpose application.

Super Hylam compact laminates have a wide range of applications like:

  • In modular Kitchens & furniture
  • Bathroom cubicles
  • Table top
  • Partitions
  • Hospital bed

We are also a renowned Manufacturers of P1 P2 and P3 grade, some of its application are:

  • Dry Type Transformer
  • LV Motors/ HV Motors
  • Control Panels
  • Power and Distribution
  • Railways
  • Aerospace
  • Mechanical application
  • Heavy Industrial Boards
  • Hydro-Generators
  • Wind Mill
